Tae Hyun Kim

researcher · engineer · energy policy specialist

전력계통 계획, 재생에너지 통합, 복원력 평가 및 에너지정책 모델링을 연구합니다. 저는 공개데이터 기반의 모델링과 투명한 정책 분석 방법론에 관심이 많습니다.

현재는 국가 전력망의 복원력 연구와 공개데이터 기반 정책수립 방법론 연구를 진행 중입니다.

Broadly, my research investigates how we can use open data and computational models to create resilient and sustainable power systems. As the energy transition accelerates, it is increasingly important to connect policy design with technological feasibility through transparent simulation and planning frameworks.

  • Builds open-source Korean power system models (PyPSA-Korea)
  • Analyzes cascading failure and resilience metrics for grid policy planning
  • Designs P2X integration and RE100 strategies for industrial complexes

Outside of work, I enjoy running simulations for fun, writing about energy economics, and exploring the intersection of engineering and policy through visual data stories.
